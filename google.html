Where can you play?

The most obvious place that you could play is at your own home with your friends coming over to play with you. However, if this isnt an option and you want more space and a great atmosphere, here are some other options. As Doktor James's homebase is in Brighton they are mostly centered there but if you google "board game shops" in your local area then they will be able to direct you to others.

Dice Saloon

Dice Saloon is an awesome warehouse full of tables to play games, as well as a great shop for buying board games and even better, a kitchen for ordering delicious pizza to eat whilst playing. Make sure you email them to book a table before showing up!
Dice Saloon

Loading Bar

Loading Bar in Brighton is a great place to play all manner of boardgames. Its right on the beach and you can order all types of food. To make it even more enticing, if you get board of playing Kastle of Katastrophe (why would you?!?) there are loads of other board games and games consoles to play with!
Loading Brighton

Ransacked

Ransacked is relatively new to Worthing and its already getting great reviews. They do vegan snacks, have loads of other board games to play and to make it EVEN MORE EXCITING, they even have some Escape Rooms to play whilst you are there!
Ransacked

Alcampo Lounge

Whilst not technically a board game cafe, it is absolutely perfect as a location for playing. Its absolutely massive and has huge tables, perfect for spreading out and playing games. As well as that it has a fully stocked kitchen and they do truly delicious food too!
Alcampo Lounge
